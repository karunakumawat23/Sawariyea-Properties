About Us


Welcome to Sawariya Property!
Aapka sapno ka ghar dhoondhna ab humara sapna ban gaya hai! Hum yeh samajhte hain ki ek ghar sirf ek jagah nahi, balki ek ehsaas hai. Sawariya Property, jiske founder Mr. Ankit Kumawat hain, aapke liye ek aise platform ka nirman karte hai jo sirf properties hi nahi, balki ek "home" ka sapna poora kare.

Our Mission
500+ customers ki khushiyon aur unke sapno ko sach karne ke baad, humara mission hai aur bhi zyada grahakon ko unke sapne ka ghar dilaana. "Har grahak ke sapne ko apni priority banakar, hum unke liye ek aise ghar ka prabandh karte hain jo sirf ek property nahi, unke jeevan ka hissa ban sake."

What We Offer
Residential Properties: Aapke budget aur pasand ke hisaab se ghar ka intezam.
Commercial Spaces: Business ke liye perfect jagah dhoondhne mein madad.
Property Consultation: Transparent aur honest advice jo aapki zarurat ke mutabik ho.
Loan Assistance: Property finance ke process ko asaan aur samajhne layak banana.

Why Choose Sawariya Property?
Trusted Experience: Real estate ke field mein humara kaam ek misaal hai.
Customer Satisfaction: Humne ab tak 500+ grahakon ke sapne ko sach kiya hai.
Personalized Service: Har grahak ki alag zarurat ko samajhna aur uske hisaab se solution dena humari pehchaan hai.
Transparency: Har deal mein poori imaandari aur clarity ka dhyan rakhte hain.

Meet Our Founder
Mr. Ankit Kumawat, ek visionary leader, jinhone Sawariya Property ka aaghaz is sapne ke saath kiya tha ki har insaan ko uska sapno ka ghar mile. Unka vishwas hai ki ghar sirf ek investment nahi, balki ek jeevan ki buniyad hai.
